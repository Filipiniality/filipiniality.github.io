Pokémon Legends: Z-A by GameFreak

The Good:

  • It has Bulbasaur.

The Bad:

  • Pokémon AI is hard to control.
  • Music is repetitive. A lot of songs were just reused from older games.
  • Plot and characters are frustrating.

I bought a Switch 2 just to play this and to be quite honest it was not worth it.
